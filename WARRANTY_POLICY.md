# Warranty Policy for the D Learning Appliance  
# Deterministic. Verifiable. Parent‑Grade. Operator‑Grade. Institutional‑Grade.

This file defines the official warranty policy for all D Learning Appliances.  
It governs coverage, exclusions, validation, refurb eligibility, and the relationship between RCA, lockdown levels, and warranty status.

A man’s got to know his limitations.  
This file defines the appliance’s.

---

## 1. PURPOSE OF THE WARRANTY POLICY
The warranty policy ensures:

- predictable support  
- parent‑grade trust  
- operator‑grade accountability  
- institutional‑grade compliance  
- reproducible refurb decisions  
- deterministic RCA validation  

Warranty must be objective, testable, and free of ambiguity.

---

## 2. WARRANTY COVERAGE
The appliance warranty covers:

- hardware defects  
- manufacturing defects  
- Golden Master image defects  
- RCA initialization defects  
- curriculum pipeline defects  
- operator‑grade assembly errors  

Coverage applies only if:

- RCA is intact  
- lockdown integrity is preserved  
- hardware profile matches RCA records  
- no unauthorized modifications occurred  

---

## 3. WARRANTY EXCLUSIONS
Warranty does **not** cover:

- physical damage  
- liquid damage  
- tampering  
- bypassing lockdown  
- unauthorized OS installs  
- unauthorized package installs  
- RCA corruption caused by user actions  
- damage caused by removing the Kensington lock  
- damage caused by forced power cycles  
- damage caused by unapproved peripherals  
- damage caused by external accounts or drift  

Any violation of lockdown rules voids warranty.

---

## 4. WARRANTY VALIDATION
Warranty is validated through RCA:

- hardware profile match  
- lockdown state match  
- RCA heartbeat  
- log integrity  
- backup integrity  
- restore‑point integrity  
- tamper‑evident seal verification (Level 3)  

If RCA is missing or corrupted, warranty is void.

---

## 5. WARRANTY PERIODS
### Level 1 — Parent‑Grade
- 12‑month hardware warranty  
- 12‑month software warranty  
- renewable with subscription  

### Level 2 — Operator‑Grade
- 12‑month hardware warranty  
- 24‑month software warranty  
- renewable with subscription  

### Level 3 — Institutional
- 24‑month hardware warranty  
- 24‑month software warranty  
- non‑renewable  
- requires physical inspection for claims  

---

## 6. SUBSCRIPTION RELATIONSHIP
Subscription provides:

- extended warranty  
- priority support  
- curriculum updates  
- RCA cloud sync (Level 1 only)  
- operator‑grade diagnostics  

Warranty and subscription are independent:

- subscription does not override exclusions  
- subscription does not override lockdown rules  
- subscription does not override RCA integrity requirements  

---

## 7. REFURB PIPELINE RELATIONSHIP
A device entering refurb must:

- pass hardware diagnostics  
- pass RCA wipe  
- pass Golden Master re‑clone  
- pass RCA re‑bind  
- pass lockdown verification  
- pass restore‑point initialization  

Refurbished devices receive:

- 6‑month hardware warranty  
- 6‑month software warranty  

If refurb fails any step, the device is recycled.

---

## 8. OWNERSHIP AND CONTROL
Warranty is tied to:

- the appliance  
- the RCA ID  
- the hardware profile  
- the lockdown state  

Warranty is **not** transferable outside the authority of Brother Bill.

---

## 9. WARRANTY TURNAROUND RULE
The software warranty guarantees correction of internal defects but does not guarantee turnaround time.  
Fixes must be delivered safely, correctly, and without compromising RCA integrity, lockdown integrity, or the Golden Master.  
No specific delivery timeline is promised or implied.

---

## 10. DEFECT PRIORITY RULE
Internal defects are corrected according to operator‑defined priority.  
Cosmetic defects (such as typos) are lowest priority.  
Functional defects (such as crashes, data loss, or RCA failures) are highest priority.  
The software warranty guarantees correction of internal defects but does not guarantee equal prioritization or urgency.  
Operator judgment determines the order of correction.

---

## 11. NO DRIFT RULE
Warranty policy must remain consistent across:

- dlang-appliance  
- dlang-school  
- from-dead-box-to-dev-box  

If one changes, all must change.

---

## 12. REPOSITORY PRIVACY AND OWNERSHIP RULE
dlang-appliance is private, not for sale, and must remain under the sole control of Brother Bill to preserve its integrity and moral purpose.
