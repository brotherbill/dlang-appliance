# Support Policy for the D Learning Appliance  
# Deterministic. Predictable. Operator‑Grade. Parent‑Grade.

This file defines the official support policy for all D Learning Appliances.  
It governs what support includes, what it excludes, how support is delivered, and how support interacts with warranty, RCA, lockdown, and the appliance boundary.

A man’s got to know his limitations.  
This file defines the appliance’s.

---

## 1. PURPOSE OF THE SUPPORT POLICY
The support policy ensures:

- predictable assistance  
- clear boundaries  
- operator‑grade diagnostics  
- parent‑grade clarity  
- institutional‑grade safety  
- deterministic troubleshooting  

Support must be consistent, reproducible, and free of ambiguity.

---

## 2. WHAT SUPPORT INCLUDES
Support covers issues **inside the appliance**, including:

- curriculum not loading  
- curriculum not running  
- workspace initialization failures  
- RCA initialization failures  
- RCA heartbeat failures  
- lockdown misbehavior  
- Golden Master defects  
- operator‑grade assembly defects  
- backup/restore subsystem defects  
- refurb‑related defects  
- internal software defects  

Support includes:

- diagnostics  
- troubleshooting  
- defect confirmation  
- operator‑grade guidance  
- safe recovery steps  

Support is for the **appliance**, not the entire computer.

---

## 3. WHAT SUPPORT DOES NOT INCLUDE
Support does **not** cover:

- Ubuntu issues outside the appliance  
- Windows issues outside the appliance  
- user‑installed software  
- user‑modified OS  
- user‑modified filesystem  
- external accounts  
- malware  
- drift  
- tampering  
- wiping the appliance  
- replacing the appliance with another OS  
- hardware damage  
- liquid damage  
- forced power cycles  
- unapproved peripherals  

If the user breaks appliance integrity, support is void.

---

## 4. SUPPORT REQUEST REQUIREMENTS
A valid support request must include:

- device serial number  
- RCA ID (if available)  
- lockdown level  
- description of the issue  
- steps to reproduce (if known)  
- whether the appliance is intact  
- whether the OS outside the appliance was modified  

If the appliance is missing or wiped, support is limited to reinstall guidance.

---

## 5. SUPPORT DELIVERY
Support is delivered through:

- operator‑grade instructions  
- deterministic troubleshooting steps  
- reproducible diagnostics  
- safe recovery procedures  

Support does **not** include:

- remote access without operator approval  
- remote access on Level 3 devices  
- modifying user data outside the appliance  
- modifying the user’s OS  
- modifying the user’s personal environment  

Support must preserve:

- RCA integrity  
- lockdown integrity  
- Golden Master integrity  
- curriculum integrity  

---

## 6. SUPPORT ESCALATION
Support escalates only if:

- RCA is intact  
- lockdown is intact  
- Golden Master is intact  
- the appliance has not been modified  
- the issue is reproducible  
- the issue is internal  

Escalation may include:

- deeper diagnostics  
- operator‑grade logs  
- curriculum pipeline verification  
- restore‑point analysis  
- Golden Master verification  

If escalation reveals tampering, support is terminated.

---

## 7. SUPPORT LIMITATIONS
Support is limited by:

- warranty status  
- appliance integrity  
- hardware condition  
- user modifications  
- lockdown level  

Level 3 devices require:

- physical operator presence  
- tamper‑evident seal verification  
- hardware profile verification  

Support cannot override lockdown rules.

---

## 8. SUPPORT AND WARRANTY RELATIONSHIP
Support and warranty are related but independent:

- warranty guarantees correction of internal defects  
- support provides assistance diagnosing and resolving them  

Support does **not** guarantee:

- turnaround time  
- speed  
- SLAs  
- customer‑dictated priorities  
- customer‑dictated timelines  

Support follows the **Defect Priority Rule**.

---

## 9. APPLIANCE BOUNDARY ENFORCEMENT
Support applies only to the appliance.

Support does **not** apply to:

- the user’s OS  
- the user’s personal files  
- the user’s personal applications  
- the user’s personal accounts  

If the user breaks the boundary, support is void.

---

## 10. NO DRIFT RULE
Support policy must remain consistent across:

- dlang-appliance  
- dlang-school  
- from-dead-box-to-dev-box  

If one changes, all must change.
