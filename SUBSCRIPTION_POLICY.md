# Subscription Policy for the D Learning Appliance  
# Fair. Predictable. Controlled. No Pressure. No Exploitation.

This file defines the subscription model for the D Learning Appliance.  
It governs pricing, activation, cancellation, fairness, growth limits, and the relationship between subscription value and curriculum pacing.

A man’s got to know his limitations.  
This file defines the subscription’s.

---

## 1. PURPOSE OF THE SUBSCRIPTION POLICY
The purpose of this policy is to:

- provide predictable access  
- preserve fairness  
- prevent exploitation  
- align subscriptions with support capacity  
- align subscriptions with curriculum pacing  
- eliminate pressure, manipulation, and scarcity tactics  

Subscriptions must be ethical, sustainable, and mission‑aligned.

---

## 2. SUBSCRIPTION INCLUDES
A subscription provides:

- access to the D Learning Appliance  
- access to all curriculum content released to date  
- access to future curriculum releases  
- access to updates, fixes, and improvements  
- access to support for appliance‑related issues  
- access to backup/restore functionality  
- access to RCA‑bound learning history  

Subscriptions do **not** include:

- support for the user’s OS  
- support for user‑installed software  
- support for external accounts  
- support for hardware damage  
- support for tampering or drift  

Subscriptions cover the **appliance**, not the entire computer.

---

## 3. PRICING RULE
Subscription pricing is:

- fixed  
- fair  
- stable  
- predictable  
- mission‑aligned  

There is **no dynamic pricing**.  
Market demand does not change the price.  
Scarcity does not change the price.

We serve families, not markets.

---

## 4. TRIAL PERIOD RULE
The trial period:

- lasts one month  
- costs $1  
- has no refunds  
- provides full access to the appliance  
- provides full access to the curriculum released to date  
- provides full access to support  

At the end of the trial:

- if the user does nothing, the subscription quietly ends  
- no further payments are charged  
- no nagging occurs  
- no pressure is applied  

All educational content delivered during the trial remains available on the device.

---

## 5. SUBSCRIPTION ACTIVATION RULE
A subscription may be activated only when:

- a device is available  
- support capacity is available  
- refurb capacity is available  
- curriculum pipeline is stable  
- Golden Master is stable  
- RCA is stable  

If capacity is exceeded, subscription activation pauses automatically.

---

## 6. QUEUE INTEGRATION RULE
Subscriptions are tied to the **queue system**.

The queue:

- holds your place  
- requires no payment  
- creates no obligation  
- guarantees nothing  
- pressures no one  

When capacity becomes available:

- the next person in line is contacted  
- they may activate a subscription or decline  

Declining does not affect their standing or relationship.

---

## 7. ONE STUDENT PER DEVICE RULE
Each device supports:

- one active student at a time  
- with the option to switch the active student once per month  

This preserves:

- RCA integrity  
- curriculum integrity  
- support capacity  
- fairness  

There is **no multi‑user sharing** of a single device.

---

## 8. CONTROLLED GROWTH RULE
Subscription growth must align with:

- support capacity  
- refurb capacity  
- operator bandwidth  
- curriculum pacing  
- Golden Master stability  
- RCA stability  

If any of these are strained, subscription activation pauses.

Growth must never exceed integrity.

---

## 9. CANCELLATION RULE
A subscription may be cancelled at any time.

Cancellation:

- stops future billing  
- does not delete curriculum  
- does not delete student progress  
- does not lock the device  
- does not disable the appliance  
- does not pressure the user to return  

The appliance remains functional, but updates and support stop.

---

## 10. REACTIVATION RULE
A cancelled subscription may be reactivated if:

- capacity is available  
- support load is acceptable  
- curriculum pipeline is stable  

Reactivation is not guaranteed.  
It depends on controlled growth limits.

---

## 11. NO EXPLOITATION RULE
Subscriptions must never:

- pressure families  
- exploit urgency  
- exploit scarcity  
- exploit fear  
- exploit status  
- exploit privilege  

Subscriptions reflect:

- moral purpose  
- educational mission  
- parent‑grade trust  
- operator‑grade integrity  

---

## 12. NO DRIFT RULE
Subscription policy must remain consistent across:

- dlang-appliance  
- dlang-school  
- from-dead-box-to-dev-box  

If one changes, all must change.

---
